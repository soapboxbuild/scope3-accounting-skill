---
description: Calculate Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions for a property or portfolio
argument-hint: [energy consumption data, location, and fuel types — or "use uploaded utility bills"]
---
Calculate the full GHG inventory for this property/portfolio:

1. Scope 1: sum all on-site fuel combustion × EPA emission factors
2. Scope 2 location-based: electricity × eGRID regional factor
3. Scope 2 market-based: electricity × applicable REC/PPA factor (or residual mix)
4. Scope 3 Cat 13: tenant energy (actual meters or floor area method)
5. Carbon intensity: tCO2e/m² and tCO2e/occupant
6. PCAF data quality score for each component
7. Year-over-year comparison if prior year data available
8. Compare against CRREM pathway target

Present as a structured GHG inventory table with methodology notes.
